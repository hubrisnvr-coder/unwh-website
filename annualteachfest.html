Grand Inauguration Highlights

The curtains rose on UnWh Innovate 2026 with thunderous applause from over 5000+ enthusiastic participants from universities across India! The event was inaugurated by distinguished guests, lighting up the stage with high-energy performances, laser shows, and a spectacular drone display forming the UNWH logo in the Himalayan sky.

This marks the beginning of an epic 3-day journey filled with cutting-edge technology, creativity, and innovation in the serene foothills of the Himalayas.
