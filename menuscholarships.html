Our Commitment to Merit

UNWH believes that financial constraints should never stand in the way of a brilliant mind. We offer a wide range of scholarships to support meritorious and deserving students.

Merit Scholarship

Based on academic performance in qualifying exams (XII / Graduation).

Uttarakhand Domicile

Special fee concession for students belonging to the hill state of Uttarakhand.

Beti Bachao-Beti Padhao

Special scholarship scheme for girl students to promote higher education.

Defence Ward

Honoring our heroes with fee waivers for wards of defence personnel.
