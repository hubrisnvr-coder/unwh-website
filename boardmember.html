Our Leadership

Guiding UNWH with Vision, Integrity, and Himalayan Wisdom

Shri Karan Malhotra

Board Member, University of North West Himalayas (UNWH)

Shri Karan Malhotra is a respected business leader, philanthropist, and education patron with more than 30 years of experience in corporate governance, strategic planning, and social impact initiatives. As a distinguished Board Member of UNWH, he brings invaluable insights from industry, ensuring the university remains aligned with real-world needs while upholding the highest standards of academic and ethical excellence.

Karan has been instrumental in guiding the university’s long-term vision, resource mobilization, infrastructure development, and global outreach. His strategic counsel has helped UNWH forge meaningful partnerships with corporates, alumni networks, and international institutions, enhancing placement opportunities, research funding, and student exchange programs.

A strong advocate for inclusive education and skill development, Shri Malhotra has championed several initiatives at UNWH including merit-cum-means scholarships, entrepreneurship incubation centers, industry mentorship programs, and community outreach projects in the Himalayan region. His focus remains on making quality higher education accessible to talented students from all backgrounds.

With a background in leading large-scale businesses and non-profit organizations, Karan combines sharp financial acumen, ethical leadership, and a deep commitment to nation-building through education. He actively participates in board deliberations, policy formulation, and performance review to ensure UNWH continues to grow as a forward-looking, sustainable, and socially responsible institution.

Shri Malhotra believes that universities must serve as bridges between tradition and modernity, knowledge and application, and individual growth and societal progress. His presence on the board strengthens UNWH’s governance with wisdom, foresight, and a genuine passion for empowering the next generation.

"Education is the greatest equalizer. As a board member, my role is to ensure that UNWH remains a place where dreams are nurtured, opportunities are created, and leaders are shaped for a better India and a better world."

With vision and responsibility,
Shri Karan Malhotra
Board Member, UNWH
