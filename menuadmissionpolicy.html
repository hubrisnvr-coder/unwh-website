Admission Policy

Transparency. Integrity. Excellence.

1. Overview of Admissions

Unity Network World Health (UNWH) University maintains a fair and transparent admission process. Admissions are granted based on merit, entrance examination scores, and eligibility criteria defined for each specific program. Our policy ensures equal opportunity for all students regardless of their background.

Note: All admissions are provisional until the verification of original documents and approval by the respective regulatory bodies.

2. General Eligibility

  • Undergraduate Programs: Minimum 50% aggregate in 10+2 from a recognized board (Relaxation for reserved categories as per Govt. norms).
  • Postgraduate Programs: Graduation in a relevant field with a minimum of 50% marks.
  • Professional Courses: Must qualify through national-level exams (JEE, CAT, MAT, etc.) or UNWH Entrance Test.

3. Application Process

Candidates can apply through the official university portal or by visiting the campus. A non-refundable application fee is mandatory for the processing of the form. Incomplete applications will be subject to rejection without prior notice.

4. Reservation Policy

UNWH follows the reservation guidelines as mandated by the State Government of Uttarakhand and Central regulatory bodies for SC/ST/OBC and EWS categories. Specific seats are also reserved for Uttarakhand Domicile students.

5. Cancellation & Refund

In case of withdrawal of admission, the refund of fees will be governed by the UGC Fee Refund Policy. The university reserves the right to deduct administrative processing charges as applicable.
