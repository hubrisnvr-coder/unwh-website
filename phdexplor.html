Doctoral Research at UNWH

The University of North West Himalayas (UNWH) offers rigorous and research-intensive Doctoral (PhD) programs designed to develop independent researchers, academicians and thought leaders.

Our doctoral programs emphasize original research, interdisciplinary collaboration and scholarly excellence under the mentorship of experienced faculty members. Scholars are encouraged to address real-world challenges through innovative research.

Research-driven disciplines across diverse fields

Advanced Computing, Software Engineering, Cyber Security & Cloud Systems

PhD in Basic & Applied Sciences

PhD in Life Sciences
