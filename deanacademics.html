Dr. Surendra Pal Singh Gurjar

Dean Academics, University of North West Himalayas (UNWH)

Dr. Surendra Pal Singh Gurjar is a visionary academic leader with over 30 years of rich experience in teaching, research, curriculum design, and higher education administration. As Dean Academics at UNWH, he spearheads the university’s academic vision, ensuring world-class quality in teaching, learning, assessment, and research output.

With a deep commitment to academic rigor and innovation, Dr. Gurjar has pioneered the adoption of Outcome-Based Education (OBE), Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS), and multidisciplinary programs aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. He has successfully led the integration of skill-based courses, value-added programs, and experiential learning to make education more relevant and future-ready.

Under his guidance, UNWH has established robust academic governance mechanisms including regular syllabus updation, faculty development initiatives, academic audits, peer reviews, and continuous quality enhancement processes. He places special emphasis on nurturing research culture, encouraging publications, patents, and interdisciplinary collaborations among faculty and students.

Dr. Gurjar holds a Ph.D. in [relevant field – e.g., Sciences / Engineering / Education] and has a distinguished record of guiding doctoral scholars, securing research grants, and publishing in high-impact journals. He has also served on various academic councils, accreditation committees, and national bodies shaping higher education policy.

His philosophy revolves around creating an inspiring academic environment where curiosity is encouraged, critical thinking is cultivated, and every student is empowered to achieve excellence. He believes that true academic leadership lies in mentoring young minds to become ethical, innovative, and socially responsible professionals who can contribute meaningfully to nation-building and global progress.

"The heart of a university beats in its classrooms and laboratories. Our mission is to ignite the spark of knowledge, foster intellectual courage, and prepare leaders who will shape a sustainable and just world."
