Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you visit or interact with The University of North West Himalayas (UNwH) website.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors, users, and others who access the UNwH website (unwh.edu.in) or any of our digital platforms. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information – Name, email address, phone number, postal address, date of birth, etc., when you voluntarily provide it (e.g., admission inquiry, registration, contact form, newsletter subscription).
  • Academic & Application Information – Details submitted during admission process, application forms, documents, etc.
  • Automatically Collected Information – IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, pages visited, time spent, referring URL, cookies, etc.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies – We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar technologies for analytics, functionality, and advertising purposes.
We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process admission applications, enrollment, and academic services
  • To communicate with you (admission updates, events, newsletters, notices)
  • To improve website functionality, user experience, and content
  • To analyze website traffic, usage patterns, and performance
  • To comply with legal obligations, university policies, and government regulations
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information only in these limited cases:

  • With university-affiliated service providers (admission software, cloud hosting, payment gateways) under strict confidentiality
  • With government authorities, courts, or law enforcement when required by law
  • With accreditation bodies, ranking agencies (NIRF, UGC, NAAC etc.) as mandated
  • In case of merger, acquisition, or transfer of university assets

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Portability of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact: privacy@unwh.edu.in

7. Cookies & Tracking

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some features of the website.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
