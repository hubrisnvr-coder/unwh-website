Department of Microbiology

Exploring the Invisible World to Solve Global Challenges.

Science of the Small

Microbiology is at the heart of medicine, agriculture, and environmental science. At UNWH, we bridge the gap between microscopic study and global application.

The Department of Microbiology offers an intensive research-based curriculum. Students explore the life of bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa, gaining insights into disease prevention, antibiotic resistance, and biotechnology. Our proximity to the Himalayan biodiversity provides a unique advantage for field research and discovery.
