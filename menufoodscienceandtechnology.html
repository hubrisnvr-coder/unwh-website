Department of Food Technology

At UNWH, we transform students into experts who ensure the safety, quality, and nutritional value of what the world eats.

The Department of Food Science and Technology provides a deep dive into food chemistry, microbiology, and processing engineering. Our curriculum is designed to meet the growing demands of the global food industry, focusing on sustainable packaging, food safety (HACCP), and new product development.
