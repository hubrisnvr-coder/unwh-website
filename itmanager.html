Our Leadership

Driving Technological Excellence at UNWH

Shubham Kumar

IT Manager & Head of Information Technology, UNWH

Shubham Kumar is a dynamic and forward-thinking IT leader with over 15 years of experience in managing enterprise-level technology infrastructure, cybersecurity, digital transformation, and academic IT systems. As IT Manager at the University of North West Himalayas, he is responsible for building and maintaining a robust, secure, and future-ready digital ecosystem that empowers learning, research, and administration.

Under his leadership, UNWH has achieved complete campus-wide high-speed Wi-Fi coverage, implemented a state-of-the-art ERP system for seamless academic and administrative operations, deployed advanced cybersecurity frameworks, and introduced cloud-based learning management systems (LMS), virtual labs, and AI-powered campus solutions.

Shubham has been the driving force behind several landmark digital initiatives including: complete digitization of student records, online examination platforms, biometric attendance & access control, smart classroom technologies, data center virtualization, and 24×7 IT helpdesk & support services. His focus remains on zero-downtime operations, data privacy compliance (GDPR, DPDP Act), and scalable infrastructure that can grow with the university.

He holds certifications in CISSP, CCNP, AWS Solutions Architect, Microsoft Azure Administrator, and ITIL, and continuously keeps himself updated with emerging technologies like AI/ML integration in education, edge computing, zero-trust security, and sustainable green IT practices.

Shubham believes that technology is the backbone of modern education. His vision is to make UNWH one of the most digitally advanced universities in the region — where every student, faculty, and staff member experiences frictionless access to resources, secure data handling, and innovative tools that enhance teaching, learning, research, and campus life.

"Technology doesn't just support education — it redefines it. At UNWH, we are building a digital foundation that is secure, intelligent, inclusive, and ready for the future."

Committed to digital excellence and innovation,
Shubham Kumar
IT Manager, UNWH
