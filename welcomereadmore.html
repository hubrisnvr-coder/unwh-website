Welcome to UNWH University

The University of North West Himalayas (UNWH) stands among India‚Äôs leading private, non-profit universities, recognized for its academic rigour, UGC-approved programs and student-centric learning environment.

Situated in the peaceful surroundings of Dehradun (Thano), UNWH offers an inspiring campus experience that blends modern infrastructure with the calm of the Himalayan foothills. This unique environment promotes creativity, focus and holistic development.

At UNWH, education goes beyond classrooms. We emphasize experiential learning, industry exposure, research orientation and global perspectives to prepare students for the evolving demands of the professional world.

Our faculty members are experienced academicians, researchers and industry experts who mentor students to achieve academic excellence while nurturing curiosity, critical thinking and ethical responsibility.

A University with Purpose

UNWH believes in nurturing future leaders who are not only experts in their respective fields but also compassionate individuals guided by strong values and ethics.

Our academic philosophy focuses on leadership development, social responsibility and lifelong learning. Students are encouraged to participate in community engagement, research projects, innovation challenges and cultural activities.

Through collaborations with industry, research institutions and global universities, UNWH ensures that students receive exposure to real-world challenges and global best practices.
