CA Neeraj Bansal

Chief Finance Officer, University of North West Himalayas (UNWH)

CA Neeraj Bansal is a seasoned Chartered Accountant and financial strategist with over 20 years of experience in financial management, budgeting, auditing, and institutional finance. As Chief Finance Officer at UNWH, he is entrusted with the overall financial health, strategic fiscal planning, resource optimization, and compliance of the university.

Neeraj has played a pivotal role in establishing robust financial systems, transparent budgeting processes, efficient fund utilization, and long-term financial sustainability for UNWH. He oversees endowment management, grant utilization, fee structuring, cost control measures, investment strategies, and statutory compliances including GST, TDS, Income Tax, and FCRA regulations.

Under his expert guidance, the university has achieved significant milestones such as: zero audit objections for consecutive years, successful implementation of ERP-integrated finance modules, timely submission of all statutory returns, optimal utilization of government grants & CSR funds, and creation of healthy reserves for future expansion and infrastructure development.

A fellow member of ICAI, Neeraj is known for his integrity, analytical acumen, and forward-looking approach to university finances. He has advised several educational institutions and trusts on financial restructuring, resource mobilization, and risk management. His focus remains on ensuring every rupee is utilized efficiently to support academic excellence, student welfare, research, and campus development.

Neeraj believes that sound financial management is the foundation of institutional growth. He works closely with all departments to align financial goals with the university’s mission of providing affordable, high-quality education while maintaining fiscal discipline and transparency.

"Finance is not just numbers — it is the enabler of dreams. At UNWH, we ensure every financial decision supports our core purpose: transforming lives through education."

With precision, transparency, and commitment,
CA Neeraj Bansal
Chief Finance Officer, UNWH
