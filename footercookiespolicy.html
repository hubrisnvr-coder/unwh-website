Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website, analyze usage, and provide personalized content. This policy explains what they are, how we use them, and your choices.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, mobile phone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Examples
Essential / Strictly Necessary Enable core website functionality like page navigation, secure login, form submission Session or persistent (up to 1 year) Session ID, authentication cookies
Performance / Analytics Collect anonymous information about how visitors use the site (pages visited, time spent, errors) Persistent (up to 2 years) Google Analytics, Matomo cookies
Functional Remember choices you make (language, region, preferences) to provide enhanced features Persistent (up to 1 year) Preferred language, dark mode setting
Targeting / Advertising Deliver relevant advertisements and measure ad effectiveness Persistent (up to 1 year) Remarketing pixels, social media trackers

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages (e.g., YouTube videos, Google Maps, social media plugins, analytics tools). These third parties may use your data according to their own privacy policies.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies – May prevent some website features from working properly
  • Delete cookies – Removes stored information (you may need to log in again)
  • Manage specific cookies – Allow or block cookies from specific sites

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies you have and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Customize your preferences
  • Reject non-essential cookies

You can change your consent at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

6. Changes to this Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
