Welcome to Petroleum Engineering & Earth Sciences at UNWH

The Department of Petroleum Engineering and Earth Sciences at UNWH focuses on the exploration, extraction, production, and management of hydrocarbons and other geological resources, alongside addressing critical environmental challenges.

Our curriculum integrates fundamental principles of geology, geophysics, reservoir engineering, and drilling technology with an emphasis on sustainable practices and advanced simulation tools. Students gain practical experience through field trips to geological sites (like the nearby Himalayan foothills) and industry-standard software training.

Led by a team of experienced faculty from both academia and industry, the department is committed to fostering innovative research in unconventional resources, carbon capture, and environmental impact assessment. Graduates are prepared for diverse roles in the energy sector, environmental consulting, and governmental agencies.
