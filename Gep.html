GEP provides a platform for students to study at leading universities in USA, UK, Canada, Europe, Australia and many more.

Through our Semester Abroad Programs (SAP) and Global Internships, UNWH students gain exposure to international teaching standards, cross-cultural environments, and global career opportunities.

International Transfer

Complete your first 2 years at UNWH and finish your degree at a partner university abroad with full credit transfer.

Summer Schools
