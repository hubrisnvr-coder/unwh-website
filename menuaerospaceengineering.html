Welcome to Aerospace Engineering at UNWH

The Department of Aerospace Engineering at UNWH is dedicated to pioneering advancements in aeronautics and astronautics. Our program provides a comprehensive understanding of aircraft design, propulsion systems, flight mechanics, and space technology.

We boast cutting-edge laboratories equipped for aerodynamics, structural analysis, and propulsion studies, including facilities for drone technology and satellite communication. Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on projects, simulations, and real-world case studies to ensure practical expertise.

Our esteemed faculty, with backgrounds from premier research institutions and industry, guide students through complex challenges. We cultivate critical thinking and innovation, preparing graduates for leadership roles in national and international aerospace organizations.
