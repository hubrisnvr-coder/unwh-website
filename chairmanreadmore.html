Our Leadership

Guiding UNWH with Vision, Integrity, and Himalayan Wisdom

Dr. Subhash Chand

President, University of North West Himalayas (UNWH)

At UNWH Dehradun, we believe education is the most powerful tool for personal, societal, and national transformation. Our vision is to develop an institution where tradition meets innovation, and where students are inspired to think independently, act ethically, and lead courageously.

We have envisioned UNWH as a modern, progressive university that upholds academic excellence, inclusivity, and sustainability. Our serene Himalayan location is not just a backdrop, but a source of calm, inspiration, and ecological consciousness. The majestic mountains surrounding us remind us daily of resilience, purity, and the importance of preserving our natural heritage while pursuing knowledge.

From the very beginning, we have focused on creating an environment that fosters holistic development. Here, students are encouraged to explore beyond textbooks — through research, community service, innovation, sports, arts, and global exposure. We integrate traditional Indian values like humility, discipline, and service to society with modern skills such as critical thinking, technology proficiency, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

Our faculty and staff are not just educators; they are mentors, guides, and role models who live the values we preach. We prioritize faculty development, research funding, international collaborations, and industry partnerships to ensure that our teaching remains relevant and impactful in a rapidly changing world.

UNWH is committed to equity and access. We provide scholarships, support for underprivileged students, gender equality initiatives, and programs for differently-abled individuals. Every student who walks through our gates should leave as a confident, responsible, and compassionate global citizen ready to contribute to a better world.

As we continue this journey, we remain guided by the belief that true education transforms not just individuals, but entire communities and nations. The University of North West Himalayas is more than an institution — it is a movement towards enlightened leadership, sustainable progress, and a harmonious future.

Together, let us build a legacy of excellence that echoes through generations.

With warm regards and unwavering commitment,
Dr. Subhash Chand
President, UNWH
