Who We Are

The University of North West Himalayas (UNwH), nestled in the serene foothills of the Himalayas in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, is a dynamic private university committed to academic excellence, interdisciplinary learning, and global leadership. Established with a vision to create a transformative educational ecosystem, UNwH empowers students through innovation, research, inclusive education, and real-world relevance.
